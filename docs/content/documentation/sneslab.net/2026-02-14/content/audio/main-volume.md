The SNES's **Main Volume** is controlled by two 8-bit registers:

- MVOLL
- MVOLR

Referring to these registers as "master volume" is discouraged because they do not affect the [Echo Volume](/mw/index.php?title=Echo_Volume&action=edit&redlink=1 "Echo Volume (page does not exist)").

### Reference

- subparagraph 7.2.2.6, [page 3-7-9 of Book I](https://archive.org/details/SNESDevManual/book1/page/n175) the official SNES development manual
