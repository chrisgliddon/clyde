**PMON** is the pitch modulation register. It is 8 bits wide, located at 2Dh. Bit 0 is unused.

### Reference

- subparagraph 7.2.2.2 of [page 3-7-7 of Book I](https://archive.org/details/SNESDevManual/book1/page/n173) of the official Super Nintendo development manual
