The **UPD6376** is a 16-bit stereo digital-to-analog converter. It is labeled "D/A CONV" in the block diagram in the official Nintendo documentation.\[1] Pins 6 (right channel output) and 11 (left channel output) are connected to the Pre-Amplifier.
