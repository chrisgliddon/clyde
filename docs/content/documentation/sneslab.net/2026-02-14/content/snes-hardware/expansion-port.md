The **Expansion Port** is the hole on the bottom of the SNES, which peripherals such as the [Satellaview](/mw/index.php?title=Satellaview&action=edit&redlink=1 "Satellaview (page does not exist)") can connect to. Pins 1-8 are connected to the SNES Bus. Pins 11~18 are connected to the CPU Data Bus.

It can send IRQs. DOTCK is the dot clock.
