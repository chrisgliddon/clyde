**MONO-OUT** is an audio line on the SNES Motherboard, as described by the jwdonal schematic. It starts by combining the signals from R66 (right stereo channel) and R69 (left stereo channel), and is fed into pin 25 of the Expansion Port. It is not connected to the Multi-Out or RF Modulator.
