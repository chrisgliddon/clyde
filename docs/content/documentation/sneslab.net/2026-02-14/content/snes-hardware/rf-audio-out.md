**RF Audio Out** is an audio line on the SNES Motherboard, as described by the jwdonal schematic. It starts by combining the signals from R68 (right stereo channel) and R71 (left stereo channel), and is fed into pin 3 of the RF Modulator. It also connects C22 (330 picofarads) to ground.
