The **Filter Amplifier**, also known as the Pre-Amplifier, receives its input signal from the Stereo DAC. At its heart are two LM345 op-amps:

- U100C, for the left stereo channel
- U100D, for the right stereo channel

Its output is sent to the Post-Amplifier.
