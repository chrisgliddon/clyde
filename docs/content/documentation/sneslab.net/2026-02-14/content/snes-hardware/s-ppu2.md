**S-PPU2** is the second of the two PPUs in 2chip consoles. It has part number 21323. Little is known about how S-PPU2 works. It performs:

- Windowing
- Mosaic
- Fades

### Legend

- 15 - color palette
- 17 - Digital-to-Analog converter for RGB output to the Darlington triad, also registers?
