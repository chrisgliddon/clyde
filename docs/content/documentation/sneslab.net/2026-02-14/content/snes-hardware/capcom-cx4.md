The **Capcom Cx4** is an enhancement chip that performs wireframe 3d graphics for *Megaman X2* and *Megaman X3*. It is among the least understood of all the enhancement chips. In scene slang, it is often simply referred to as "C4."

Cx4 opcodes are 16-bits wide.
