Basic Info **Addressing Mode** **Opcode** **Length** **Speed** Implied (type 2) 80 1 byte 2 cycles

Flags Affected N V P B H I Z C . . . . . . . 1

**SETC** is an SPC700 instruction that sets the carry flag.

No other flags are affected.

#### Syntax

```
SETC
```
